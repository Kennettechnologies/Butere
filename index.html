WHO WE ARE

Dedicated to Providing Healing & Hope

Originally established to provide essential healthcare services, our facility has grown under the Kakamega County Government. We support our patients with a modern laboratory, digital radiology, dedicated maternity wards, pediatric clinics, and emergency services.

HEALTH TIPS

Empowering You with Better Health Knowledge

Prevention is always better than cure. Our clinical team regularly publishes health tips and guidelines to keep your household healthy, active, and safe.

  • Regular Screening: Check blood pressure and sugar levels at least once every six months to detect hypertension or diabetes early.
  • Adequate Hydration: Drinking 2-3 liters of clean water daily helps maintain kidney function and promotes smooth digestion.
  • Malaria Prevention: Sleep under insecticide-treated mosquito nets and clear stagnant waters around your residence.
