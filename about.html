OUR HISTORY

Over Two Decades of Dedicated Service

Butere County County Hospital was established with the vision of offering the highest standard of diagnosis, treatment, and medical counseling to the community of Kakamega County.

Beginning as a minor local health center in the early 2000s, the institution has undergone significant facility upgrades, expansion of bed capacities, and digitization of medical records. Under the direct stewardship of the County Government of Kakamega, we serve over 150,000 patients annually from Butere, Mumias, Khwisero, and beyond.

Today, Butere County County Hospital is a certified county facility, complete with specialized theatres, neonatal units, fully automated diagnostic labs, digital radiology centers, and a comprehensive care clinic (CCC) for chronic conditions.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in county healthcare delivery, recognized for quality patient outcomes, innovative services, and compassionate caregivers.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality, customer-focused, and affordable healthcare services to the residents of Butere County and surrounding regions through teamwork and technology.

Core Values

  • Compassion & Care: Putting patient dignity first.
  • Integrity: Honesty and professional ethics.
  • Professionalism: Highly trained and accountable.
  • Innovation: Adopting modern technology.
TIMELINE

Our Key Milestones

A history of constant growth, improvements, and dedication to saving lives in Western Kenya.

2005

Establishment of local health center

First set up as a small community clinic providing basic primary care, child immunizations, and outpatient consultations.

2013

Upgraded to Sub-County Hospital

Following devolution, the Kakamega County Government expanded the facility to build a 50-bed ward, basic maternity theater, and a laboratory.

2019

Digital Upgrade & Lab Automation

Replaced paper medical records with an integrated hospital information management system. Installed automated lab analyzers and digital X-ray suites.

2025

New Maternal & Neonatal Block

Commissioned a state-of-the-art maternal block with dedicated operating theaters, recovery wards, and a neonatal intensive care unit.

Our Institutional Structure

Butere County County Hospital operates under the Department of Health Services, Kakamega County Government. We maintain collaborative ties with the National Ministry of Health and Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Council (KMPDC).

1. Hospital Board

Includes community stakeholders, county health directors, and administrative chairs making strategic decisions.

2. Executive Committee

Led by the Superintendent and Administrator, handling daily clinical policies and financial activities.

3. Wards & Departments

Direct clinical care units staffed by specialists, registrars, nurses, and laboratory scientists.
