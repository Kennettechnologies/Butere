Outpatient Department (OPD)

Our General Outpatient Department serves as the entry point for most of our clinical services. We have multiple doctors' consulting rooms to ensure minimal waiting times.

Services Rendered:
  • Clinical Consultation by Registered Medical Officers
  • Triage, blood pressure monitoring, and temperature evaluation
  • Treatment of minor ailments and infections
  • Observation rooms for stabilizing patients
Head of OPD: Dr. David Wafula | Hours: 24/7 Hours open
Maternity & Neonatal Department

We pride ourselves in being one of the safest delivery hubs in Butere Sub-County. Our dedicated maternity ward is equipped to handle emergency obstetric situations.

Services Rendered:
  • Normal & Assisted Vaginal Delivery
  • Cesarean section operations in our specialized theatre
  • Antenatal Clinic (ANC) every Monday and Thursday
  • Neonatal care including baby incubator support
Lead Midwife: Sister Joyce Auma | Hours: 24/7 Hours
