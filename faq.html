SHA Coverage

Yes, we fully accept SHA cards for both outpatient checkups and inpatient admissions. Kindly present your active insurance card at the records and billing counters for verification.

Yes, normal deliveries, cesarean operations, prenatal clinic (ANC) checks, and infant child immunizations are covered 100% under the government Linda Mama initiative. Registration is handled on site.

Appointments & Consultations

You can schedule a consultation online using our Appointment Portal or by dialing +254 (0) 700 000 000. You will receive an SMS confirmation showing your clinical department and arrival slot.

Yes, general outpatient consultations (OPD) and emergency triage run on a walk-in basis 24 hours a day. However, planned consultant clinics (such as orthopedic reviews or gynecological surgery prep) require booking.

Visiting Wards & Hours

Patient visiting hours are strictly scheduled between **1:00 PM and 2:00 PM** and **4:30 PM and 6:00 PM** daily. This structure guarantees that clinical rounds, medications, and ward cleaning are executed smoothly.

To maintain hygiene, prevent congestion, and guarantee patient comfort, only **2 visitors** are allowed at a patient's bedside at any given time.

Payments & Fees

We accept payments made via government M-Pesa Paybill systems, visa cards, and county revenue receipt desks. General cash payments are not accepted at doctor consulting offices for transparency reasons.

Yes, emergency life-saving stabilization is administered immediately to all patients, regardless of upfront payments. Our county medical social workers collaborate with families to resolve billing later.
