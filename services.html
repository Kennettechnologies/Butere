OUR CLINICAL OFFERINGS

Core Health Services

Consultation Services

Diagnostics & Ultrasound

Equipped with digital radiography systems and ultrasound analyzers to support accurate and swift skeletal and prenatal diagnostics.

Pharmacy Dispensing

SERVICE FAQS

Common Inquiries

Quick answers regarding service charges, NHIF/SHIF registration requirements, and clinic operating hours.

Linda Mama is a public funded program ensuring pregnant women access free maternity care. When you register with your ID or ANC booklet at our front desk, all prenatal visits, ultrasounds, normal/cesarean delivery, and infant immunizations are completely free.

You need to provide your active NHIF/SHIF card, a valid national identification document (or birth certificate for minors), and a lab request slip filled out by our consulting clinician. Pre-authorization is handled at the billing desk.

Child Welfare and KEPI Immunization services are active from Monday through Friday between 8:00 AM and 4:00 PM. Weekend emergency immunizations (such as anti-rabies, tetanus toxoid) are dispensed through the emergency room pharmacy.
Consultation Services

General clinical consults are open 24/7. Specialized clinics run on weekdays, booking via the records desk is advised. Specialist consultancy includes Pediatrics, Surgery, Orthopedics, and Gynecology.

Diagnostics & X-Ray

We operate digital chest and limb radiography. Ultrasounds are handled by qualified sonographers. Reports are returned to the clinical database within minutes.

Lab Screenings

Our automated lab machinery handles biochemistry panels, complete blood count (FBC), renal panels, liver tests, blood typing, malaria test, and stool evaluations.

Maternity Deliveries

Our maternity unit offers safe normal deliveries, high-risk birth supervision, and neonatal intensive support. Covered by Linda Mama.

Child Welfare

Child development checks, growth curves, nutritional counselling, vitamin A supplement rounds, and infant dietary advice.

KEPI Immunizations

Provides BCG, Oral Polio Vaccine (OPV), Pentavalent vaccine, Pneumococcal Vaccine (PCV), Rotavirus Vaccine, and Measles-Rubella vaccine under KEPI.

Emergency & Ambulance

24-hour ambulance team, trauma response unit, shock and burn treatment. Dial emergency hotlines for swift county dispatch.

Pharmacy Dispensing

Dispenses essential medication. Provides patient counselling regarding drug usage patterns, medication schedules, and side effect indicators.
