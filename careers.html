Download & Fill Out Forms

Select the appropriate PDF form, print it, and fill out all details including dates and clinical targets.

Attach college logs, recommendation letters from school department chairs, and national identification card copy.

Send scanned copies to careers@buterehospital.go.ke or deliver physically to room 102 in the administration building.
